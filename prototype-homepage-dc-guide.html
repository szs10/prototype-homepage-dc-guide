Where should I study?

Aka coffee shops and study spots for when you need a change of scenery.

  • Bakers & Baristas
    • Independently own coffee shop well located for Judiciary Square and surroundings.
    I do love that this little place delivers on two things I want done well: coffee and pastries.
    The vibe here is definitely hipster and you can tell it's and older place, but ultimately it's
    cozy and has a lot of natural light in a central location. Learn more here.
  • A Baked Joint
    • This is the ultimate reputable DC coffee spot and bakery that fully lives up to its level of hype.
    They have a large assortment of drinks, pastries and other baked goods. Although it is in a bit
    of a less happening neighborhood near union station, it's worth going out of your way for the
    bread and coffee here. Learn more here.
  • Le Pain Quotidien
    • It's a massive space made of wood with a lot of natural light. You can either walk up to the
    bakery and order a cup of coffee and chocolate croissant or you can wait to be seated in one
    of their many booths. The only bad thing is that it is a little pricy. Learn more here.
